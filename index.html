I am currently offering private Final Cut Pro lessons at a very affordable rate.

About Me


I edit movies, TV shows and promos.

Since moving to New York, I have been working with some of the top names in Post Production in New York City. I started out as the 2nd Assistant Editor on the feature film "Sons of Sakhnin," an official selection of the Tribeca Film Festival in 2007. I have worked on a variety of projects ranging from political ads during the 2006 Democratic House and Senate Races and Hillary Clinton for President 2008. Since then, I have assisted two-time Emmy winner Patrick Gambutti, Jr. and Kurt Engfher, editor of the films Bowling for Columbine and Farenheit 9/11 for Michael Moore on the feature film "At the Edge of the World," a feature length documentary featuring animal activists defending Japanese whalers from illegally slaughtering whales in the Antarctic Whale sanctuary. "At the Edge" was featured in the 2008 Toronto International Film Festival.
